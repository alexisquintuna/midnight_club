Where It All Began

Han Seoul (founder)

Han Seoul is the founder of the Midnight Club. It all started back when he was in Chicago and was introduced into the underground scene of cars. Here he saw a variety of cars. Starting from show cars to race cars to drift cars. He saw it all and was amazed. Night after night he saw more and more cars but everytime the group went out to show of the cars it'll always be shut down by cops. This made him think what if there was a way to still organize such events but in a more disclose area. This is when he invented the Midnight Club. A car group where theres nothing but love to each other and cars, a little competion here and there to see whos the real big dog but nothing too intense.
